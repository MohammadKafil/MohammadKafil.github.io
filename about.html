BRITISH COLUMBIA PETROLEUM

Dubai, United Arab Emirates, UAE.

Established In 1983

British Columbia Petroleum was founded in the emirate of Dubai, United Arab Emirates and first licensed on 1983 to trade in commodities. After a decade of trading the company progressed to development with its first project of constructing an Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) Plant. Today, after an aggresive expansion through mergers and acquisitions, British Coloumbia Petroleum has extensive knowledge in all areas relating to hydrocarbon chemistry and engineering which spans across upstream, midestream and downstream business..

"Post Hoc, Ergo Propter Hoc"

Our History

1983-1999

Commodity Trading

Trading license in commodities where the main business was to act as a "middle-person" between two counterparties.

1999-2004

ABS Plant Project


The first development project: To construct an ABS plant that was in cooperation with experienced counterparts in Taiwan. Completed and delivered ahead of schedule.

2004-2013

Acquisition of Knowledge


The expansion of knowledge in hydrocarbon chemistry, petroleum engineering, mechanical engineering, civil engineering, financial engineering, information technology, risk management and retail distribution.
